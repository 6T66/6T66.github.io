Why Would Anyone Want to Read Something so Depressing?

I honestly have no idea. This book is very long and drawn out, furthermore, certain types of people have
tendencies to cry and I bet not alot of people in the world want to hear a sad story right now. You could
get a complete run down on certain history websites such as Commonsense.org. If you are looking
for a love story however, this would probably just the thing for you! Overall, this book is not to my liking
simply because I am not into drama and other things concerning the human emotion of "Love" in books.
Now someone could argue that this is a good way to learn what different people went through but, the purpose
here is to inform rater than entertain. So no, I wouldn't recommend this book to anyone as it is extremely
time wasting in terms of the overall efficiency on learning about the subject.
